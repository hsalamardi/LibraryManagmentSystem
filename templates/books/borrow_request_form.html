Frequently Asked Questions
How long does approval take?

Most requests are processed within 24 hours during library operating hours.

Can I cancel my request?

Yes, you can cancel pending requests from your "My Books" page.

What happens after approval?

The book will be reserved for you and you'll receive pickup instructions via email.

Can I extend the borrowing period?

Extensions can be requested through the librarian before the due date.
